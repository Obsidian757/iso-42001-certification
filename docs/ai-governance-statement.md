## Our AI Governance Principles

### 1. Human-Centric AI
We design AI systems that augment human decision-making, not replace human judgment. Every AI solution we deliver includes meaningful human oversight.

### 2. Transparency & Explainability
We document how our AI systems work, what data they use, and what limitations they have. Clients receive clear explanations of AI-driven outputs.

### 3. Security First
As a company founded by a Navy veteran with active DoD Secret clearance, we apply defense-grade security to all AI implementations. Zero trust architecture is our default.

### 4. Compliance by Design
We build regulatory requirements (NIST AI RMF, OMB memos, ISO 42001) into every AI system from day oneâ€”not as an afterthought.

### 5. Continuous Accountability
Every AI system has a named owner. Every decision is traceable. Every incident is investigated and learned from.

### During Operation

### Continuous Improvement

## Third-Party AI We Use

We leverage industry-leading AI platforms (Claude, ElevenLabs, Vapi, Azure AI) with enterprise-grade security and compliance certifications. All third-party AI vendors undergo annual risk review.

**Our commitments for third-party AI:**
- No client confidential data shared without explicit consent
- No use of consumer-grade AI for sensitive workloads
- Clear data processing agreements with all vendors
- Backup plans for vendor failures

## Your Rights

When you work with 12th House AI:

- **Right to Explanation:** You can request an explanation of any AI-driven recommendation
- **Right to Appeal:** You can challenge AI outputs and request human review
- **Right to Data:** You own your data. We delete it when you say so.
- **Right to Compliance Documentation:** We provide audit-ready documentation for your compliance needs
