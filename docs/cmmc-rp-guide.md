## What is CMMC RP?

A **CMMC Registered Practitioner** is an individual authorized by CyberAB to provide consulting services, assisting organizations in preparing for CMMC certification.

**What RPs CAN do:**
- Interpret CMMC requirements for clients
- Identify compliance gaps
- Develop mitigation strategies
- Prepare organizations for certification assessments
- Charge premium rates for CMMC consulting

**What RPs CANNOT do:**
- Conduct official CMMC assessments
- Issue CMMC certifications
- Sign off on assessment findings

**Perfect for consulting:** You advise, they get certified by a C3PAO.

## RP Certification Process

### Step 1: Register with CyberAB
**Website:** https://cyberab.org

**Requirements:**
- Must be citizen of USA, Australia, South Korea, or NATO country âœ…
- Pass commercial background check
- Affiliate with RPO or work as independent contractor

**Your path:** Register as independent (you are 12th House AI)

### Step 2: Pay Application Fee
**Cost:** $600 (one-time application fee)

### Step 3: Complete Background Check
**Cost:** ~$35
**Time:** 3-5 business days typically

### Step 4: Complete Online Training
**Format:** Web-based, self-paced
**Content:** 12 modules covering:
- CMMC model overview
- Ecosystem roles (RPs, C3PAOs, etc.)
- Assessment process
- Appeal processes

**Quizzes:** Each module ends with a quiz
- 80% pass rate required
- 2 attempts per day, 6 attempts max per quiz

**Time to complete:** 1-2 days focused, or spread over 1-2 weeks

### Step 5: Sign Agreements
- CyberAB Code of Professional Conduct
- RP Agreement

### Step 6: Certification Issued
You're now listed in CyberAB directory as a Registered Practitioner.
