## Instructions

For each requirement:
- **C** = Conforming (fully meets requirement)
- **OFI** = Opportunity for Improvement (meets but could be better)
- **NC-m** = Nonconformity - Minor (isolated issue, does not impact AIMS effectiveness)
- **NC-M** = Nonconformity - Major (significant gap or systematic failure)
- **N/A** = Not Applicable (with justification)

Record evidence examined and any observations.
