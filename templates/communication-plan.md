# AI Governance Communication Plan

**Document Control**
- Version: 1.0
- Effective Date: February 1, 2026
- Owner: Brannon Solomon (CEO)
- Review Frequency: Annual

---

## Purpose

This plan defines how 12th House AI communicates about AI governance matters to internal and external stakeholders, ensuring compliance with ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Clause 7.4 (Communication).

---

## Communication Objectives

1. **Transparency:** Keep stakeholders informed about AI governance practices
2. **Compliance:** Meet regulatory notification requirements
3. **Trust:** Build confidence in 12th House AI's responsible AI practices
4. **Engagement:** Enable stakeholder feedback and participation
5. **Awareness:** Ensure all parties understand their AI-related responsibilities

---

## Communication Matrix

### Internal Communications

| What | Who | When | How | Owner |
|------|-----|------|-----|-------|
| AIMS Policy updates | All personnel | Within 5 days of approval | Email + document repository | CEO |
| Quarterly governance review results | All personnel | Within 1 week of review | Team meeting + summary email | CEO |
| AI incident notifications | Affected team members | Immediately upon detection | Slack/SMS + follow-up email | Technical Lead |
| Training requirements | New personnel | Within 30 days of hire | Onboarding checklist + training platform | CEO |
| Risk register updates | Relevant personnel | Monthly | Document repository notification | AI Risk Owner |
| Procedure changes | Affected personnel | 2 weeks before effective | Email + document update | Procedure owner |

### Client Communications

## Communication Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Audience | Response Time |
|---------|---------|----------|---------------|
| **Email** | Formal communications, documentation | All stakeholders | 24 hrs (business days) |
| **Phone** | Urgent matters, relationship building | Clients, partners | Immediate for Critical |
| **Slack** | Internal team coordination | Internal (when team grows) | 4 hrs (business hours) |
| **SMS** | Critical incident alerts | On-call personnel | Immediate |
| **LinkedIn** | Thought leadership, announcements | Public, prospects | N/A (publishing) |
| **Website** | Public information, governance statement | Public | N/A (static) |
| **Client Portal** | Project deliverables, status | Active clients | Per SLA |
| **Document Repository** | Policies, procedures, evidence | Internal, auditors | N/A (access-based) |

## Notification Requirements by Incident Type

| Incident Type | Internal | Client | Regulator | Public |
|---------------|----------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Data Breach (PII)** | Immediate | 24-72 hrs | 30-60 days (state law) | If 500+ affected |
| **Data Breach (PHI)** | Immediate | 24-72 hrs | 60 days (HIPAA) | If 500+ affected |
| **Data Breach (CUI)** | Immediate | 24 hrs | 72 hrs (DoD) | No |
| **AI System Outage** | Immediate | Per SLA | No | No |
| **Bias Incident** | Immediate | 48 hrs | If regulatory impact | If public impact |
| **Security Incident** | Immediate | 24-48 hrs | If breach confirmed | No |
| **Compliance Finding** | Immediate | If affects client | If required | No |
