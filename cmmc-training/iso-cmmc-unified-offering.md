## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do we really need both CMMC and ISO 42001?**

A: If you handle CUI AND use AI, yes. CMMC protects the infrastructure; ISO 42001 governs the AI. AI systems processing CUI need both.

**Q: Can we do CMMC first, then ISO 42001 later?**

A: Yes, but it costs more and takes longer. Integration saves $15K-$40K and 2-3 months.

**Q: What if we only need CMMC Level 1?**

A: Level 1 is for basic safeguarding (no CUI). If you don't handle CUI, you may not need CMMC at all. We can adjust the scope.

**Q: How does this relate to NIST AI RMF?**

A: ISO 42001 and NIST AI RMF are aligned. Our ISO 42001 implementation satisfies NIST AI RMF requirements.

**Q: What if we fail the assessment?**

A: Tier 3 includes POA&M support and remediation. Most clients achieve certification on first attempt with proper preparation.

**Q: Can you work with our existing IT/security provider?**
