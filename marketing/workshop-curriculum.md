## Learning Objectives

By the end of this workshop, participants will be able to:

1. **Identify** all AI systems in their organization (including Shadow AI)
2. **Understand** federal AI requirements (OMB, NIST, CMMC, ISO)
3. **Assess** AI risks using standardized methodologies
4. **Create** foundational AI governance documentation
5. **Plan** their 90-day path to AI compliance

## Facilitator Notes

### Key Discussion Prompts:

1. "What AI tools are your employees using that you might not know about?"
2. "If a auditor asked to see your AI governance documentation today, what would you show them?"
3. "Which AI system poses the highest risk to your federal contracts?"
4. "What's stopping you from addressing AI compliance today?"

### Common Participant Concerns:

| Concern | Response |
|---------|----------|
| "We don't use AI" | Review definition, check for embedded AI, discuss ChatGPT usage |
| "This is overwhelming" | Focus on 90-day plan, prioritize high-risk systems first |
| "We don't have budget" | Start with free templates, build business case from risk |
| "Leadership doesn't prioritize this" | Provide executive briefing materials, contract risk data |
