## CADENCE RECOMMENDATION

**New prospects:**
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up if no response
- Day 7: Add on LinkedIn if not connected
- Day 14: Final follow-up with value add
- Day 30+: Re-engagement if still relevant

- Day 2: Send promised resources
- Day 3-5: Schedule call
- Day 7+: Follow-up on call insights

---

## PERSONALIZATION TIPS

1. **Reference something specific** - Recent award, LinkedIn post, company news
2. **Keep it short** - 100-150 words max for cold emails
3. **Ask one question** - Make it easy to respond
4. **Provide immediate value** - Free tool or insight
5. **No pitch in first email** - Build relationship first
